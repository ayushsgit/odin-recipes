Cheese Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 green onions, finely chopped
  • 4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • Â¼ teaspoon garlic salt, or to taste

Directions

Step 1

Mix mayonnaise, sour cream, green onions, blue cheese, dried parsley, and garlic salt in a medium bowl until combined.

Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving.
